Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 24th, 2024

At OneOneThree Digital ("we", "our", or "us"), your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website (https://www.oneonethree.co.nz), purchase our products, or use our services.

By using our website, you consent to the practices outlined in this policy.

1. What We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact information: such as your name and email address (only if you voluntarily opt in or contact us).
  • Order details: including your shipping address, phone number, and product preferences when you make a purchase.
  • Payment information: processed securely by Stripe; we do not store full credit card details.
  • Device and usage information: including your IP address, browser type, and activity on the site (collected via cookies and analytics tools).

2. How We Use Your Information

We may use your personal information to:

  • Fulfill and deliver orders
  • Provide customer support
  • Send you updates or marketing emails (only if you have opted in)
  • Improve our website and marketing based on visitor behaviour
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

3. Cookies & Tracking Tools

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience and gather analytics. This includes:

  • Google Analytics 4: to understand user interactions and website performance
  • Meta Pixel: to help us serve relevant ads and measure campaign effectiveness

You can control cookie settings through your browser at any time.

4. Email Marketing

If you opt in to receive marketing communications, we may use your email to send product updates, special offers, and news. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the link in any email or contacting us directly.

5. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information. Data is stored securely using reputable third-party platforms (such as Stripe, Shopify, or analytics services) that comply with modern data security standards.

6. Your Rights

As a New Zealand resident, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing at any time

To make a request, please contact us at the email below.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites and recommend reviewing their privacy policies before sharing personal information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.
